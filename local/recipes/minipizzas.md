<h3>Ingredients</h3>

<p>This makes 4 mini pizzas</p>
 
<div class="bullets">
<div class="ingredients">
<ul>
<li>2 Sandwich Thins</li>
<li>Pesto (green or red)</li>
<li>Grated Cheese</li>
<li>Pepperoni</li>
<li>Tomato chopped</li>
<li>Italian Herbs</li>
<li>Any alternative or other toppings you want!</i>
</ul>
</div>


<!-- Method -->
<h3>Method</h3>
<div class="method">
<ol>
<li>Preheat your oven to around 200-230C</li>
<li>Split each sandwich thin into two halves and place face up on a piece of baking paper </li>
<li>spread some pesto on each slice</li>
<li>sprinkle grated cheese on top</li>
<li>add your toppings (pepperoni, tomato etc)</li>
<li>sprinkle italian herbs on top</li>
<li>cook in the oven until golden brown</li>
<li>Enjoy!</li>
</ol>
</div>

<!-- Notes -->
<h3>Notes</h3>
<div class="notes">
<ul>
<li>I cook mine in a toaster oven. you may or may not need to adjust the temperature if you cook in a regular oven or do them under a grill.</li>
<li>you can put whatever ingredients you want on them, these are just how I like them right now</li>
<li>If you can't get sandwich things for any reason, this works just as well with a english breakfast muffin, or a slice of bread, or a mini pizza base, or a pocket bread etc. Anything flat and breadish.</li>
<li>except maybe a wrap, I have tried with wraps and they just come out soggy. your mileage may vary though.</li>
<li>Mine usually cook in around 5 mins or so, at around 230C in my toaster oven, sometimes I turn the air fryer feature on to speed it up. </li>
</ul>
</div>
