Sharing some interesting youtube videos I watched and some great channels I subbed to in December 2025

#### Subbed to:   

[Jet Lag The Game](https://www.youtube.com/@jetlagthegame/videos)   
Super fun travel channel where a group of friends travel to different countries playing tag, hide and seek, capture the flag etc across an entire country. They utilise a deck of cards they have created (and sell) that have challenges and curses to help or hinder them as they travel. 

Its a little bit like amazing race, but only on a smaller scale and with different gameplay elements.

I first found the channel via one of their hide and seek videos and watched the entire season then went back and started watching every season. My favourite game I think is hide and seek where one person hides somewhere in the country and the others have to find that person using special game play elements to narrow down the location, as they travel around looking for the hider. I love when someone gets hit with a curse and they have to do something wacky in order to continue, and the challenges are always entertaining. 

Its an interesting way to see a country, where its just the real country as they travel through it, and not filtered and instagramed to look fake etc. You see how the trains work, smaller more rural areas, locations off the beaten path etc but also some more touristy spots. 

Definitely worth watching imo!
