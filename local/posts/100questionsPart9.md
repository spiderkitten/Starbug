So, I found this questionnaire which was originally created by Mouseling.net (you can find it [here](https://mouseling.net/100webmaster)) and I thought I would try and answer some of the questions. 

I'm breaking it up into parts though, so its an easier read. 

<strong>This is Part 9 questions 81 - 90</strong>

<dl>
<dt><strong>81. Would you run a website with another person? How would that work?</strong></dt>
<dd>no, I've done that before and while it was fine, I think I'm too much of a control freak now. I'd only be able to do it if we had our own logins and content we created ourselves was protected from deletion by the other person. I've seen too many friendships go bad and people get removed from their own sites to do it I think</dd>

<dt><strong>82. Do you surf the Web to find new personal websites very often?</strong></dt>
<dd>yes! quite often, although not everyday (or every week) but I do like going to a random site checking it out, then finding another to visit via their button wall</dd>

<dt><strong>83. Do you bookmark other people's websites? How would you feel knowing someone else bookmarked yours?</strong></dt>
<dd>I do, I had quite a lot bookmarked at one point but recently cleared them out and linked some on my links page. I have no issue with people bookmarking my site, why would that even be a problem? it means they want to come back to later! and anyway, its not my business what people choose to bookmark</dd>

<dt><strong>84. What do you want people to be most impressed with when they see your website?</strong></dt>
<dd>I don't think I need anyone to be impressed, I just want people to visit and find something interesting to them.</dd>

<dt><strong>85. Are you interested in technology outside of websites? Do you collect?</strong></dt>
<dd>depends on how you define technology but I have reached the age now where I don't understand some technology, and also do not want some technology. I'm not sure what the collect question means, collect what? collect technology?</dd>

<dt><strong>86. How often and for how long are you online?</strong></dt>
<dd>too often, too long lol</dd>

<dt><strong>87. When it comes to your website, who is your target audience?</strong></dt>
<dd>I am my target audience. I make my site mostly for me, and if anyone comes along and finds enjoyment from it thats a bonus.</dd>

<dt><strong>88. Have you ever been interested in XHTML?</strong></dt>
<dd>not really, its too strict for me</dd>

<dt><strong>89. Do you program in general? Have you ever written a program for use with or on your website, not counting simple JavaScript?</strong></dt>
<dd>nope! and nope! </dd>

<dt><strong>90. Speaking of programs that help you make websites, what do you think of static site generators (SSGs)? Have you ever used one?</strong></dt>
<dd>I love them! I use 11ty to help with my site. I write all the code - the header, footer, menu and content, but they are all in seperate files and 11ty joins them together into a single file for me. makes it easy to update things just once instead of on every page!</dd>
</dl>
