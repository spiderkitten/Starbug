Sharing some interesting youtube videos I watched and some great channels I subbed to in December 2025

#### Subbed to:   

[Tara Farms](https://www.youtube.com/@TaraFarms/videos)   
Tara is a third generation sheed and crop farmer who makes hilarious youtube videos about daily farm life on a 4000 acre farm in Victoria, Australia. Tara is a sheep farmer who is raising her own flock of Aussie Whites and Primelines. We see her everyday from checking the water supply, fixing the fences, collecting eggs, weighing the sheep, herding the sheep etc. 

Tara has a very Australian sense of humour, and is very honest about farm life in Australia. She is raising some sheep to sell for meat, and its very eye opening as to how the Australian lamb industry works. But mostly the videos are full of Tara being funny, her working dogs, and and general every day life. Definitely worth a watch! 

[Back From Burnout](https://www.youtube.com/@backfromburnout/videos)  
Mel is an Australian ebay bookseller who makes daily vlogs about her online bookselling business. She is a talker, so while she tells us about her business we see her in her warehouse picking and packing, sorting books, and generally doing book selling things. 

Its really interesting to learn how ebay works for book sellers, what books sell, her thrift store finds, and about her everyday life as a bookseller. We also sometimes see content about her partner who is an online seller of Movies and Music, and how he runs his business which is on a much larger scale. 

[ActionAdventureTwins](https://www.youtube.com/@ActionAdventureTwins/videos)  
Twins who go caving! If small cramped spaces terrify you then this channel is probably not for you! The guys head into caves both mapped and unmapped and push as far as they go through tunnels, cracks, narrow spaces etc. Crawling through low spaces, squeezing through tiny gaps, going down and down. 

The guys don't really talk to the camera, just record their trips. But its still really interesting to see how even deep down in caves there has been a human precense for many many years. 


[Hairy Tornado](https://www.youtube.com/@HairyTornado/videos) 
The Hair Tornado channel is a husband and wife team who make youtube videos about their reselling business. They film their trips to goodwill, the goodwill bins, flea markets and other places, and also share content about how they sell their finds, both on ebay, whatnot and in their booth. 

The couple have great personalities and are able to negotiate great deals, which they then turn into profit. Its lots of fun to see all the things they buy and learn about how their business works. They also talk some about their new property and what they are doing to improve their land.
