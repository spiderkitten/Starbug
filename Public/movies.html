Movies

One of my favourite things to do is to curl up on the couch with a good movie. Especially on a rainy weekend. I could spend all weekend watching movies. When I was younger I would go to the movies all the time, even on my own. These days I watch via streaming services as there isn't a cinema nearby.

Favourite Movies

There are sooooo many movies I love. I tried to make a list just for my own reference really, but also in case anyone wants some movie recs.

But in short, I love: Horror, Sci-Fi, Mind Fucks, Folk Horror, Post Apocalypse, Rankin Bass christmas movies, Shirley Temple movies, Doris Day movies, Time Travel, Korean Horror, and more.
