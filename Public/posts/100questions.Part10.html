100 Questions for Webmaasters (Part 10/10)

January 15, 2026
Tags: blog

So, I found this questionnaire which was originally created by Mouseling.net (you can find it here) and I thought I would try and answer some of the questions.

I'm breaking it up into parts though, so its an easier read.

This is Part 10 questions 91 - 100 - The last part!

91. Do you keep a hitcounter? Why or why not?
there is one, but its on my site credits page, I use it as for a tiny joke, you'll have to look to see what it is lol
92. Do you frequent forums? Which ones?
no, I am a member at a few but rarely visit
93. Do you write your page content directly into the editor, or do you prepare it elsewhere, like a text document or a Word document?
I write my pages locally on my computer and then upload them to neocities via github
94. Do you think you appear cool to others? A more accurate answer now: do other people ever say you're cool?
no. and no.
95. Are you embarrassed of your old work? Have you ever deleted everything out of shame?
I have... but it was all learning, and I have improved since then
96. Would you close down your website if you couldn't update it, or would you leave an archive?
I'm not sure why I wouldn't be able to be update it... but I would probably take it down, or move it somewhere so I could update it!
97. Do you reveal a lot about yourself on your website? Or are you more secretive?
I mean, I let people know a little about me and what I enjoy but otherwise I like to keep things to myself. I don't think its being secretive, its just valuing my privacy.
98. Are you willing to reveal who your best online friend is, and/or if they have a website?
nope!
99. And do you optimise the images on your website?
I have optimised SOME images but not all images. Anything really big is optimised.
100. We're out of time! How do you feel after answering 100 questions? ....other than exhausted.
some of the questions were fun, others a bit baffling, a few were a bit repeatitive. It was interesting to think about some of my answers though!
