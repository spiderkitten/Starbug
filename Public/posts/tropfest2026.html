Tropfest 2026

Tropfest is back!

For the first time 7 years Tropfest, an Australian short film festival is back and was held in Centennial Park in Sydney. Every year (that its held) submissions open to anyone who wants to enter, whether you are a proffessional film maker or not. All you need is a camera (phone or otherwise) and an idea.

I'm a HUGE fan of short film, and I watched the event live online on youtube, and there were some great short films!

Each year there is a different Signature Item that is named that people need to somehow work into their short films. The Signature Item for 2026 was Hourglass It was really neat to see how people worked the Hourglass into their films.

Tropfest Official Site
Tropfest Youtube Channel
2026 Tropfest Awards Show on youtube (just under 4 hours)

Films Presented

Out of over 700 entries there were 15 finalists which were decided by the Awards' panelists. There were also 10 wildcard films that competed for the 16th spot which was decided by youtube viewers. Of the 10 wildcards, the film with the most engagement won the spot.

Here is the list of finalists with links to youtube to watch the short films!

  • Communicate by Frazeir Brockett
  • Crescendo by Lianne Mackessy
  • Eat Now Pay Later by Dimitri Ellerington
  • Faker by Clare Sladden
  • Gazers by Veialu Aila-Unsworth and Sze Lok Ho
  • I'm Still Here by Catho D'Souza
  • No Thank You by Georgina Haig
  • Project Hourglass by Benjamin Mathews
  • Ring Around by Sisi Stranger
  • Silent Night by Nicky Tyndale-Biscoe
  • Syd Confidential by Clinton J Isle (AI)
  • The End by Sean Bayles
  • Tilly by Luke Mayze
  • Unprompted by Stephen Packer
  • We Don't Take Breaks by Jasper Sharpe
  • Blue Orchids by Taysha McFarland (Wildcard)

I'm choosing not to link to Sydney Confidential as it was entirely AI generated and should not have been a finalist imo.

  • Project Hourglass

Winners

The winners for this year were:

First Prize

Crescendo by Lianne Mackessy

Second Prize

We Don't Take Breaks by Jasper Sharpe

Third Prize

Silent Night by Nicky Tyndale-Biscoe

Other Awards

Best Female Actor: Laura Bunting (Crescendo)
Best Male Actor: Ben Keller (We Don't Take Breaks)
Creative Acceleration Award: No Thank You
Best Cinematography: Josh Flavell
Women's Brilliance Award: Silent Night
Tropicana Award: The End
People's Choice Award: has not been decided at time of writing

2027 Signature Item

Every year at the awards they name the Signature Item for the following year. This year the item named for 2027 was "Necklace", so people need to work a necklace into their films in some way. I can't wait to see how!
