100 Questions for Webmaasters (Part 4/10)

January 7, 2026

So, I found this questionnaire which was originally created by Mouseling.net (you can find it here) and I thought I would try and answer some of the questions.

I'm breaking it up into parts though, so its an easier read.

This is Part 4 questions 31 - 40

31. Do you prefer simple or highly visual websites?
that really depends on the design, but generally speaking I prefer simple
32. Do you stick to certain colours? Do you do that on purpose, or is it your subconscious?
I do not, in fact my current layout which uses pinks, greens and reds is not like me at all lol
33. Have you ever thought about quitting? Why?
hmmm once or twice but its more to do with some bad apples in some communities, people who want to gatekeep things or who are rude to newbies.
34. Do you have many webmaster friends, or is it a solitary hobby?
this is mostly a solitary hobby for me, but I am generally a solitary person in general, not just for this hobby
35. Do people in your real life know about your website?
my immediate family does, but no one else does
36. Do you update your website very often? How often is "very often"?
I update it randomly. I'll sometimes go weeks without updating, other times I'll update 5 times in one day. Depends on what I am working on and if I feel its a good point to upload or not
37. And the overall design, do you change that much? Why or why not?
I used to change it quite often, because its fun to make sites, but my current layout I haven't changed in a while
38. Is your website more you-focused, hobby-focused, or outside world-focused?
I think its more me focused, but I would like to make it a bit less about me and more about other things, but that will happen with time
39. Do you do web design professionally?
Nope. I did want to at one point, back in the 90s/2000s. But not now.
40. If not, would you like to? And if you're comfortable answering, what do you do for work?
I would not want to now. I'm also not going to talk about what I do for work.
