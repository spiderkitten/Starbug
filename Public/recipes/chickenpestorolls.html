Ingredients

  • 2 cold cooked chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup pesto (red or green)
  • 1 tbsp of greek yoghurt or smooth ricotta
  • 140g (half a small jar) or roast capsicum or sun dried tomato

    • Optional Ingredients:

  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • smooth ricotta
  • bread rolls, sliced bread or sandwich thins

Method

  1. dice or shred the cold chicken breasts
  2. drain, and pat dry (with paper towels) half the jar of roast capsicum or sun dried tomatoes
  3. In a bowl, mix your pesto and yoghurt (or ricotta) together until combined
  4. add the chicken and roast capsicum (sun dried tomato) to the mix and stir until well coated
  5. make up your rolls with lettuce and anything else you want on it (or just make a salad)
  6. spoon some of the chicken mix onto the roll. The rest can be kep in the fridge in a container for a couple of days.
  7. Enjoy!

Optional: its also nice served up with hot chips on the side!

Notes

  • Note that if you refridgerate the mix there will be a small amount of oil seperation from the capsicum/tomato but if you drained and dried it first it shouldn't be very much and you can stir it back into the mix.
