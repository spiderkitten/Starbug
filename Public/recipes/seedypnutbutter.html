Seedy Peanut Butter

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cups Roasted Peanuts
  • 2tbs Pepitas
  • 2tbs Sunflower Seeds
  • 1tbs Flaxseeds
  • 1tbs Sesame Seeds
  • 1tbs Chia Seeds
  • Sesame Oil (optional)
  • Salt (optional)

Note: makes around 500g of peanut butter

Method

  1. In a food processor, blend all the peanuts until smooth (see notes)
  2. While that is blending, in a seperate bowl or cup add all the seeds and mix together
  3. When Peanut Butter is smooth, add the seeds, and pulse once or twice
  4. Store in an air tight container on the shelf, or in a container in the fridge

Notes

  • To prevent overheating your food processor try blending for a short period, and then resting it for a minute or two to allow the moter to cool down a little.
  • -
  • You can use less peanuts, for a smaller amount of peanut butter, but you probably need to at least make sure there are enough peanuts to just cover the blades to ensure the process works.
  • In my experience 2.5 cups of peanuts makes around 500g of peanut butter.
  • -
  • It will take several minutes for the peanuts to get smooth. It will look like it isn't working but then it will magically get smooth. Usually the progress is: whole peanuts > crumbed > powdered > paste > ball > grainy smooth > smooth
  • Be careful not to over process or it will be a bit runny.
  • -
  • The colour of your peanut butter will depend on how dark the roasted peanuts are. Raw unroasted peanuts = pale peanut butter.
  • If you use salted peanuts you may not need to add salt, but taste the PB and see if it needs it.
  • -
  • You can always add a little sesame oil to the mix for an oiler PB or for taste. But the PB should be very smooth from the blending even without the oil.
