Lazy Slowcooker Spicy Beef

Ingredients

And by spicy I mean, faily mild lol

  • Mince Meat (Ground Beef)
  • Frozen diced onion
  • Frozen red capsicum (bell pepper)
  • frozen spinach
  • frozen brocolli
  • 1 packets of chow mein seasoning

the amounts don't really matter, the kind of vegies don't really either. just throw in however much you want of whatever you want. I usually use just eyeball it, and fill up the bowl with whatever I have.

Method

  1. Throw everything except the brocolli in the slow cooker
  2. add in the spice mix and mix it in
  3. then add the brocolli on top (its easier to stir without the frozen brocolli)
  4. Cover and leave to cook on high for around five hours

Notes

  • If you are using a lot of frozen veg this may get liquidy so you may need to drain it a little. The liquid is tasty though so you could save it and put it in a soup or use it make a gravy or something.
  • Can served with rice, or just eaten as is.
  • Its also nice on a wrap with some lettuce and cheese but its messy this way.
