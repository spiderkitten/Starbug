Microwave Porridge

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup Rolled Oats
  • 2/3 - 3/4 Cup Milk (any kind, I use almond)
  • any mix ins like peanut butter, chocolate, fruit, honey etc

Method

  1. In a microwave safe bowl add the oats and milk.
  2. Microwave for around 3 minutes
  3. Remove from microwave and stir in your mix ins
  4. Enjoy!

Notes

  • You can use any kind of milk. I prefer almond milk, myself.
  • My favourite mix-in is Peanut Butter, but sometimes I will stir in some chocolate drops as a treat
