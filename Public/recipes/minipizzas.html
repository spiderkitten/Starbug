Mini Pizzas

Ingredients

This makes 4 mini pizzas

  • 2 Sandwich Thins
  • Pesto (green or red)
  • Grated Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Tomato chopped
  • Italian Herbs
  • Any alternative or other toppings you want!

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to around 200-230C
  2. Split each sandwich thin into two halves and place face up on a piece of baking paper
  3. spread some pesto on each slice
  4. sprinkle grated cheese on top
  5. add your toppings (pepperoni, tomato etc)
  6. sprinkle italian herbs on top
  7. cook in the oven until golden brown
  8. Enjoy!

Notes

  • I cook mine in a toaster oven. you may or may not need to adjust the temperature if you cook in a regular oven or do them under a grill.
  • you can put whatever ingredients you want on them, these are just how I like them right now
  • If you can't get sandwich things for any reason, this works just as well with a english breakfast muffin, or a slice of bread, or a mini pizza base, or a pocket bread etc. Anything flat and breadish.
  • except maybe a wrap, I have tried with wraps and they just come out soggy. your mileage may vary though.
  • Mine usually cook in around 5 mins or so, at around 230C in my toaster oven, sometimes I turn the air fryer feature on to speed it up.
