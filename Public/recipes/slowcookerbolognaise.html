Lazy Slow Cooker Bolognaise

Ingredients

I usually eyeball the ingredients and don't measure. So for this recipe I've listed approximately how much I usually put in, but feel free to adjust for your needs.

  • mince/ground beef - I'll use 100-200g grams
  • diced tomato - I use 1 can
  • diced onions - I usually use two smaller or one big one
  • spinach - I use about 100g of frozen spinach
  • sliced carrots - I use around 200g of frozen carrot strips
  • broccoli rice - I fill the rest of the slow cooker up with it
  • pasta sauce - just to thicken and add some colour
  • garlic powder - I just shake it across the top
  • italian herbs - I use a lid full
  • Salt - just a quick shake

Method

  1. just dump everything in a slow cooker
  2. cook on high for 6 to 7 hours
  3. serve!
  4. or spoon into containers. keeps in the fridge for a few days, or longer if frozen

Notes

  • I use frozen veggies. the carrots and spinach are store bought but I make and freeze the brocolli rice myself
  • to make brocolli rice, just put a head or two of fresh brocolli into a food processor for about 30 seconds to a minute or until riced. then put it in a freezer safe ziplock or container and freeze.
